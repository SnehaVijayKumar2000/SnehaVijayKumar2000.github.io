Aishwarya Vijay Kumar

Born and brought up in Bangalore, Karnataka. I am 18 years old and currently doing my 12th grade(ISC Arts) at Sophia High School.
I want to be in the art field because personally I always gravitate towards art and design and love to be creative. Out of all the available options out there I chose to do animation and become an animator because I like to bring my characters to life and be able to expand my imagination and thoughts in reality. The process behind every animated movie is so fascinating to me and I wish to be able to do all this myself.

Happiness in small deeds

Every year during navratri me and my family members go out to the most downward areas of Bangalore and try and feed as many people we can . This act of kindness is small to us but this act is greatly appreciated by the people who need this the most . We are able to fill the hungry stomachs of the needy and the joy on their faces is so pure and our hearts feel light. This is one of the best ways to help the ones in need because food is a necessity to all .

Gateway of India Mumbai a monument in Maharashtra

The Gateway of India is an arch-monument built in the early 20th century in the city of Bombay now known as Mumbai, India. It was erected to commemorate the landing of King-Emperor George V, the first British monarch to visit India, in December 1911 at Strand Road near Wellington Fountain. I recently visited Mumbai and got the opportunity to visit the Gateway of India and I captured this beautiful picture of this marvelous monument.

The Taj Mahal Palace, Mumbai

The Taj Mahal Palace is a 5-star hotel .It has been considered one of the finest hotels in the East since the time of the British Raj.The hotel was one of the main sites targeted in the 2008 Mumbai attacks. This picture was also captured when i visited Mumbai in 2022.
